Privacy Policy — Thrive Dating

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Thrive Dating ("Thrive", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application. By creating an account or using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who We Are

Thrive Dating is an independently operated dating application currently in private testing. The app is not yet publicly available through the App Store or Google Play. Testing is limited to invited participants. For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at stump865+privacypolicy@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & profile information

  • Email address and password (stored and authenticated through Supabase)
  • Profile details you provide: name, age, gender, pronouns, orientation, bio, preferences, and interests
  • Profile photos you upload
  • Onboarding answers you share with our AI assistant ("Cupid") during account setup

2.2 Location data

  • Your approximate and precise location when the app is open, and — if you grant permission — in the background
  • Location is used to suggest nearby matches and recommend local date spots. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings

2.3 Microphone and audio

  • When you use voice chat with our AI assistant ("Cupid"), we capture your microphone audio and send it to our backend for processing and real-time AI conversation
  • Audio is processed by OpenAI under their Realtime API and is not retained in raw form on our servers beyond what is necessary for the active session

2.4 Camera and photo library

  • When you upload a profile photo, the app requests access to your camera or photo library to select an image. Only images you explicitly select are uploaded

2.5 Messages and interactions

  • Messages you send to matched users
  • Messages you exchange with our AI assistant ("Cupid")
  • Matches, likes, blocks, reports, and date recommendations

2.6 Device & usage information

  • Push notification token (if you opt in to notifications)
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Basic crash and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To match you with other users based on profile data, preferences, and location
  • To power our AI assistant ("Cupid"), which helps with onboarding, conversation, and date recommendations
  • To generate personalized date spot suggestions using nearby points of interest
  • To send you push notifications you have opted in to
  • To enforce our community standards (e.g., acting on reports, blocking abusive users)
  • To debug and improve the app

4. Third-Party Services

We share the minimum information required for these services to function:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage. Privacy policy.
  • OpenAI — powers our AI assistant ("Cupid"), profile description generation, and date recommendation logic. Conversations and related text data are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. Privacy policy.
  • Google Maps Platform / Google Places — used to display maps and fetch nearby venue data for date spot suggestions. Location may be sent to Google in the course of these requests. Privacy policy.
  • Apple Push Notification Service / Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — used to deliver push notifications if you have opted in.
  • Railway — hosts our backend server that brokers requests between the app and OpenAI / Google services. Privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers.

5. Data Retention

  • Profile and account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Messages are retained for as long as both participants' accounts exist
  • If you delete your account, we remove your profile, photos, and messages from our active systems. Residual copies may remain in encrypted backups for a limited period before being purged

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: You may update your profile at any time within the app
  • Deletion: You may request full deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at the address below
  • Permissions: You may revoke location, microphone, camera, photo library, or notification permissions at any time in your device settings
  • Blocking: You may block or report other users directly within the app

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) for all communication between the app and our backend. Passwords are hashed by Supabase and are never stored in plaintext. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

Thrive Dating is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us at the address below and we will delete it.

9. International Users

The app's backend and third-party processors (Supabase, OpenAI, Google, Railway) may process your data in the United States or other countries. By using the app, you consent to this transfer and processing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, data access requests, or deletion requests, email us at stump865+privacypolicy@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
